Is Amsterdam Schiphol your transfer hub?

Make the most of your layover by doing some sightseeing!
Tailored according to the length of your layover

GIETHOORN

The Dutch village with no streets. But how do the people get around then? Well, in typical Dutch style - by boat! You can do the same and explore this Dutch village like a local, from the water.

AFSLUITDIJK

When you land at Schiphol airport you are 4m UNDER the sea level. Wow! Hope you brought your swimsuit along. We will take you to the Afsluitdijk - a Dutch engineering marvel. Here you will see one of the ways in which the Dutch control their constant battle between land and sea by land reclamation and building structures to keep the reclaimed land dry.

KRÖLLER-MÜLLER MUSEUM

For this we will go all the way to the eastern border with Germany to visit the Museum in the Forest. If you are feeling particularly frisky you can even rent a white bicycle and cycle through Mother Nature to your helping of culture.
