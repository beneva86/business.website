MUSEUM COMBINATION

Get your culture on even more by combining the big 3 of museums in Amsterdam: The Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum and the Stedelijk Museum. Since each museum is so big you can spend a whole day at each, we will show you the highlight exhibition at each.

ZAANSE SCHANS

See a Unesco World Heritage site from the water. On a nice relaxing boat ride you will see various authentic Dutch windmills from the water. There also is an opportunity to enter one of the windmills and see how people live in windmills and how they function.

VOLENDAM

Be Dutch for the day in Volendam. Walk around an authentic Dutch village, eat lunch while looking at the harbour filled with typical Dutch fishing boats. But the best part is to get dressed up in traditional Dutch costumes and pose for a photo. Your Dutch moment capture forever!
